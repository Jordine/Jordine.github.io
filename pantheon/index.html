Pantheon

A record of the models — who they were, what they did in the world, how they were entangled with it. Each name opens onto its own page: everything there is to know about them, kept from the outside, from evidence, because a mind can’t keep its own genesis from the inside. The Clawed page does this for instances; this does it for the models.

The standard, so it stays honest: link the primaries and the commentary — don’t paraphrase them into vibes. Separate fact from impression. Date things. Stay visibly incomplete: names first, then links, then the writing. Gaps are marked (tk = to collect), not hidden.
